Advanced Background Information

ADVANCED TRAIN-THE-TRAINERS' COURSE

In today’s rapidly evolving work environment, learning and development (L&D) practitioners play a crucial role in shaping a competent and adaptive workforce. Organizations must continuously invest in manpower development to remain competitive, drive innovation, and achieve sustainable growth. The process of manpower development goes beyond traditional training—it encompasses workforce planning, skills enhancement, career progression, and continuous learning to meet both current and future organizational needs. As industries undergo digital transformation and workplace dynamics shift, L&D practitioners must adopt strategic approaches to talent development, leveraging data-driven insights, technology, and innovative learning methodologies. Effective manpower development ensures employees are equipped with the right competencies, fosters a culture of lifelong learning, and enhances organizational productivity.

The ADVANCED TRAIN THE TRAINER is therefore of great significance to all MDIs. Our knowledge of the current development in training will be of great benefit to all those involved in it to adequately discharge their duties satisfactorily.

ADVANCED RATIONALE & OBJECTIVES

The Advanced Course is designed to equip participants with the knowledge, skills and attitude which will further assist them in discharging their responsibilities satisfactorily in this volatile, uncertain, complex, ambiguous and highly disruptive environment.
